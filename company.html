SD Capital Systems is building a marketplace for Canadian fixed income securities with a focus on increasing access to bonds, convertible bonds and preferred shares for Canadian retail investors.

The fixed income securities offered in the marketplace, including analytical tools at both the individual security and portfolio level, include:

  • Government of Canada Bonds
  • Government of Canada Real Return Bonds (i.e. Inflation-Linked Bonds)
  • Canada Mortgage Bonds
  • Canadian Provincial Bonds
  • Canadian Municipal Bonds
  • NHA MBS
  • Investment Grade (IG) Corporate Bonds
  • High Yield (HY) Corporate Bonds
  • Floating Rate Bonds
  • Convertible Bonds
  • Preferred Shares
