SD Capital Systems Inc.

SD Capital Systems is building a marketplace for Canadian fixed income securities with a focus on increasing access for Canadian retail investors. The securities offered in the marketplace, including analytical tools at the individual security level and portfolio level, include:

  • Government of Canada Bonds
  • Government of Canada Real Return Bonds
  • Canada Mortgage Bonds
  • Canadian Provincial Bonds
  • Canadian Municipal Bonds
  • NHA MBS
  • IG Corporate Bonds
  • Floating Rate Bonds (of above products)
  • Convertible Bonds
  • Preferred Shares
